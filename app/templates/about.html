About AgriSense

Technical details and project background

Problem Statement

SIH 2022 · SG426

Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers' Welfare — Development of an AI-based system to detect crop diseases from leaf images and provide actionable recommendations for farmers, including treatment protocols and preventive measures.

Crop diseases cause an estimated 20-40% annual loss in global agricultural production. Early detection can save billions in crop losses and improve food security for millions of farmers.

Our Approach

Data Collection

PlantVillage dataset — 54,000+ images of diseased and healthy crop leaves across 38 classes.

Data Augmentation

Random crops, flips, rotations, color jitter, perspective transforms, and random erasing to improve generalization.

Transfer Learning

EfficientNet-B0 pre-trained on ImageNet with frozen early layers and custom classification head.

Training Strategy

AdamW optimizer, label smoothing (0.1), cosine annealing scheduler, and gradient clipping for stable convergence.

Knowledge Base

Curated disease database with pathogen info, symptoms, treatment protocols, and fertilizer recommendations.
